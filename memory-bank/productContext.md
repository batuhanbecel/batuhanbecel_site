# Product Context

## Purpose
Showcase Batuhan Becel's professional retouching work and career journey to attract creative directors, agencies, and brands.

## Problems Solved
- Provides a centralized, professional online presence
- Displays portfolio work in an elegant, distraction-free manner
- Communicates experience and expertise effectively

## User Experience Goals
- **First impression**: Clean, professional, memorable
- **Navigation**: Intuitive, smooth scrolling between sections
- **Portfolio viewing**: Seamless lightbox experience with keyboard navigation
- **Performance**: Fast loading, optimized images
- **Responsive**: Perfect on all devices

## Target Audience Needs
- Quick access to portfolio work
- Understanding of professional background
- Easy way to connect via social platforms
